Working With Python

In this project, I was given 369,712 rows of example voter data to use to analyze election results and audit the Colorado Congressional Election.

My end objectives were to calculate the total number of votes cast and obtain a complete list of candidates who received votes. I was also tasked to enumerate the total number of votes each candidate received, the percentage of votes each candidate won with and ultimately determine the winner of the election based on popular vote,
